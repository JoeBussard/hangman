JavaScript Practice Project 3

Building a hangman game

The plan for this website is as follows
I'll write a hangman game where a user enters the secret word.
The word will be displayed in underscores as in _ _ _ _ _ _ .
The user's friend will take over and try to guess the word one letter at a time.
Correct letters will replace underscores _ O O _ _ _
Wrong letters will decrement number of guesses and will be placed in their own container.
When the word is complete, we'll increment "Hi-Score" and enter a new secret word.
